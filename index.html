About

We all know that for new graduates, it's particularly difficult to find a job these days; we need the latest open positions and a variety of information to prepare for interviews. My own experience has taught me that looking around online for all this news and information can be very time-consuming and requires having a dozen or so websites open on your computer at all times. Therefore, my peers and I were in desperate need of a platform to aggregate news and information to help us in our job finding.
